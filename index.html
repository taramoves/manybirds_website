UNDERSKY

Fulldome Performance · 2025

A future-historical reenactment of Earth's flora and fauna, long after we've gone.

UNDERSKY is a fulldome performance staging a future-historical reenactment of Earth's flora and fauna long after humanity has departed. Built in Unreal Engine using public-domain scientific illustrations animated and transformed with generative AI, the work imagines immersive entertainment as both a memorial to and a mediation of ecological loss, situated within the dome's legacy of scientific spectacle.

About

MANYBIRDS is a Montreal-based audiovisual performance collective founded in 2025 as a vessel for ongoing collaboration between new media artist Tara Rose Morris and musical composer Graham Steinman. The collective explores immersive performance as a site of ecological memory, speculative futures, and sensory entanglement—where sound and image behave less as representation and more as environment.

Tara Rose Morris

Traditional animation inside game engines; traversable three-dimensional collaged landscapes; live audiovisual systems; misrecognition and ecological estrangement.

Graham Steinman

Augmented field recordings; hybrid acoustic–synthetic composition; auditory worlds that blur organic and artificial sound.
